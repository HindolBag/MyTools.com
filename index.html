What is Compound Interest Calculator?

A compound interest calculator is a simulation, that shows how investments grow with time. You need three parts to calculate the compound interest that is the principal amount, interest rate, and time for which the money is invested.


How to Calculate Compound Interest?

Groww uses a globally standardized method to determine the total compound interest accrued. The formula is:

A = P (1 + r/n) ^ nt

P - Principal Amount
A - Compound interest
R/r - Rate of interest
N/n - Number of times interest compounds in a year
T/t - Number of years
